Hotel Les Hibiscus: Where Cinema Meets Coastal Elegance

Just steps from one of Senegal’s most stunning beaches and close to Cap Skirring International Airport, Hôtel Les Hibiscus offers a lush, tranquil retreat. Set within expansive tropical gardens, the hotel features a 100-seat performance hall and a panoramic bar-restaurant with a terrace and pool overlooking the ocean.

Its 65 elegant rooms and suites, spread across charming bungalows, are each dedicated to a legendary film star — uniquely styled in their honour. Open year-round, the hotel blends cinematic flair with natural beauty and timeless comfort.

Imagine...

Bask in the sun year-round on a pristine 28-kilometre beach, perfect for long walks, cycling, quad biking, or even exploring by pirogue.

The warm, inviting waters (25–28°C) are ideal for swimming or surfcasting, offering both relaxation and adventure.

All around, lush, flowering vegetation surrounds you—ancient baobabs and kapok trees tower overhead as a symphony of birdsong completes the scene.

Getting Here

Whether arriving by air, sea, or road, reaching Hôtel Les Hibiscus / Mer is part of the adventure. We’re located just minutes from Cap Skirring International Airport in the peaceful village of Kabrousse.

Arriving by Air

Direct flight Paris → Cap Skirring: ~6h via Transavia, available Wednesdays and Sundays.

International flights to Dakar: Corsair, Air France, Iberia, Brussels Airlines, Royal Air Maroc, TAP and others.

Domestic connection: Daily flights from Dakar to Cap Skirring (~45 minutes) or Ziguinchor, followed by a short transfer.

By Ferry from Dakar

Three weekly ferries run by COSAMA operate the route Dakar → Ziguinchor (with a stop in Karabane). Approximate travel time: 18 hours.

Ferry Aline Sitoé Diatta Schedule:

  • Dakar → Ziguinchor: Tuesdays & Fridays — 20:00 departure, 10:00 arrival next day
  • Ziguinchor → Dakar: Thursdays & Sundays — 13:00 departure, 07:00 arrival next day

From Ziguinchor: Taxi or shuttle to the hotel (1 hour).

Tickets: Available at ferry terminals in Dakar or Ziguinchor.

Activities & Excursions

Discover the magic of La Casamance — a region of untamed beauty, endless beaches, and unforgettable sunsets. Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or cultural immersion, Hôtel Les Hibiscus / Mer offers a gateway to it all.

🌿 Nature & Cultural Discovery

Venture out to explore the Diola villages, where tradition and heritage thrive. Walk along untouched beaches, glide past mangroves by pirogue, or journey inland by 4x4 to discover sacred forests, rice fields, and the rhythm of rural life.

Each season brings new colors and flavors to experience. Guided hikes, village visits, and storytelling excursions offer an authentic glimpse into the soul of Casamance — vibrant, rooted, and full of life.

💆‍♀️ Wellness & Relaxation

Rejuvenate by our luxurious pool or right on the beach, where our bar team will serve you fresh fruit cocktails, smoothies, or a chilled glass of rosé.

Reconnect with your senses through soothing spa treatments, massages, and esthetic care — or greet the sunrise with gentle yoga by the sea.

🎯 Sport & Adventure

From sunrise to sunset, experience the thrill of motion and the joy of play with an exciting selection of activities:

  • Beach volleyball, ping-pong, pétanque, and tennis courts
  • Thrilling water sports like kite-surfing, boogie boarding, and jet skiing
  • Off-road fun with ATV, quad bikes, and scenic VTT trails
  • Group fitness sessions, aquagym, and energizing dance workshops

🌙 Evenings Under the Stars

As the sun dips below the horizon, Hôtel Les Hibiscus transforms into a magical stage. Feel the rhythm of Casamance with live music, drumming circles, and mesmerizing open-air cinema beneath a canopy of stars.

Celebrate the night with themed dinners, artisan markets, cultural performances, and barefoot dancing in the sand — or simply relax in a starlit lounge, cocktail in hand, as the ocean hums its lullaby.
